# Challenges in Cryo-ET Data Collection

- **Dose & Damage**  
  Low electron dose = low signal-to-noise.

- **Missing Wedge**  
  Limited tilt range (±60° or ±70°) leads to incomplete angular sampling.

- **Beam-Induced Motion**  
  Movements at high tilt angles degrade resolution.
